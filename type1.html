Composition Basics

Made with Adobe Illustrator

16"x20"

Inspired by the principles of Constructivism, this project explores the dynamic interplay of form and function through a strikingly asymmetric composition. Utilizing a diverse selection of fonts from the American type classification—including Old Style, Transitional, Modern, Slab Serif, Sans Serif, and Script—each element is carefully orchestrated to create a visual rhythm that challenges traditional hierarchies in typography. The integration of letters, numerals, and punctuation fosters a sense of movement and energy, inviting viewers to engage with the artwork on multiple levels.
