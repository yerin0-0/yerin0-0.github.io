Poster Design Final

Made with Adobe InDesign

8.5” x 24”

This final poster represents the culmination of an intensive and technical creative process, refined through multiple rounds of review and feedback. Each element has been meticulously crafted, reflecting a deep commitment to precision and clarity in design. The poster's layout demonstrates a sophisticated understanding of visual hierarchy, balancing text and imagery to effectively communicate complex information. The iterative nature of this project highlights the importance of collaboration and critique in the artistic journey, as insights from peers and mentors have shaped the final outcome of different works that fall under the theme of Medium Over Message in book design.
